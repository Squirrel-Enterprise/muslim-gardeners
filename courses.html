Connecting with Nature; Little Muslims
$10 per Child
For Groups or Organizations:
This series of classes teaches children how to connect with Nature and find God in his creations. They will learn the life cycle of plants, what is the soil made of, what are pollinators and why is it important, how to plant and grow seeds, and much more depending on how many weeks you decide to book. Activities may include tasting, planting, meditation in nature, plant and tree identification and more. $10/Child per hour, $2 discount for additional sibling.
Your group decides how many classes to offer.
